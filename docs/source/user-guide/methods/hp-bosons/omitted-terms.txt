In the Hamiltonian we omit the terms which are smaller then the
:math:`1/S^2` (if one assume :math:`S_a = S_b = S`). In other words we omit the terms
with more than four bosonic operators. Here are those terms:

.. math::
  \dfrac{1}{4S_aS_b^{3/2}}
  \Bigl[
    &(\boldsymbol{f}_a)^{\dagger}
    \boldsymbol{\tilde{J}^{mdab}}
    \boldsymbol{p}_b
    \cdot
    \hat{a}_{ma}^{\dagger}\hat{a}_{ma}\hat{a}_{m+d_{ab},b}^{\dagger}\hat{a}_{m+d_{ab},b}^{\dagger}\hat{a}_{m+d_{ab},b}
    +
    (\boldsymbol{f}_a)^{\dagger}
    \boldsymbol{\tilde{J}^{mdab}}
    \boldsymbol{t}_b
    \cdot
    \hat{a}_{ma}^{\dagger}\hat{a}_{ma}\hat{a}_{m+d_{ab},b}^{\dagger}\hat{a}_{m+d_{ab},b}\hat{a}_{m+d_{ab},b}
    +\\&+
    (\boldsymbol{p}_a)^{\dagger}
    \boldsymbol{\tilde{J}^{mdab}}
    \boldsymbol{f}_b
    \cdot
    \hat{a}_{ma}^{\dagger}\hat{a}_{ma}\hat{a}_{ma}\hat{a}_{m+d_{ab},b}^{\dagger}\hat{a}_{m+d_{ab},b}
    +
    (\boldsymbol{t}_a)^{\dagger}
    \boldsymbol{\tilde{J}^{mdab}}
    \boldsymbol{f}_b
    \cdot
    \hat{a}_{ma}^{\dagger}\hat{a}_{ma}^{\dagger}\hat{a}_{ma}\hat{a}_{m+d_{ab},b}^{\dagger}\hat{a}_{m+d_{ab},b}
  \Bigr]
  +\\+
  \dfrac{1}{16S_a^{3/2}S_b^{3/2}}
  \Bigl[
    &(\boldsymbol{p}_a)^{\dagger}
    \boldsymbol{\tilde{J}^{mdab}}
    \boldsymbol{p}_b
    \cdot
    \hat{a}_{ma}^{\dagger}\hat{a}_{ma}\hat{a}_{ma}\hat{a}_{m+d_{ab},b}^{\dagger}\hat{a}_{m+d_{ab},b}^{\dagger}\hat{a}_{m+d_{ab},b}
    +
    (\boldsymbol{p}_a)^{\dagger}
    \boldsymbol{\tilde{J}^{mdab}}
    \boldsymbol{t}_b
    \cdot
    \hat{a}_{ma}^{\dagger}\hat{a}_{ma}\hat{a}_{ma}\hat{a}_{m+d_{ab},b}^{\dagger}\hat{a}_{m+d_{ab},b}\hat{a}_{m+d_{ab},b}
    +\\&+
    (\boldsymbol{t}_a)^{\dagger}
    \boldsymbol{\tilde{J}^{mdab}}
    \boldsymbol{p}_b
    \cdot
    \hat{a}_{ma}^{\dagger}\hat{a}_{ma}^{\dagger}\hat{a}_{ma}\hat{a}_{m+d_{ab},b}^{\dagger}\hat{a}_{m+d_{ab},b}^{\dagger}\hat{a}_{m+d_{ab},b}
    +
    (\boldsymbol{t}_a)^{\dagger}
    \boldsymbol{\tilde{J}^{mdab}}
    \boldsymbol{t}_b
    \cdot
    \hat{a}_{ma}^{\dagger}\hat{a}_{ma}^{\dagger}\hat{a}_{ma}\hat{a}_{m+d_{ab},b}^{\dagger}\hat{a}_{m+d_{ab},b}\hat{a}_{m+d_{ab},b}
  \Bigr]

.. note::
  In order to account for more than 4 operator terms one need to expand the square root
  in the HP transformation up to higher degrees in :math:`1/S`.

The last two omitted terms are one-operator terms, proportional to the :math:`1/\sqrt{S}`:

.. math::
  \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{S_a}}
  \Bigl[
    (\boldsymbol{p}_a)^{\dagger}
    \boldsymbol{\tilde{J}^{mdab}}
    \boldsymbol{f}_b
    \cdot
    \hat{a}_{ma}
    +
    (\boldsymbol{t}_a)^{\dagger}
    \boldsymbol{\tilde{J}^{mdab}}
    \boldsymbol{f}_b
    \cdot
    \hat{a}_{ma}^{\dagger}
  \Bigr]
  +
  \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{S_b}}
  \Bigl[
    (\boldsymbol{f}_a)^{\dagger}
    \boldsymbol{\tilde{J}^{mdab}}
    \boldsymbol{p}_b
    \cdot
    \hat{a}_{m+d_{ab},b}^{\dagger}
    +
    (\boldsymbol{f}_a)^{\dagger}
    \boldsymbol{\tilde{J}^{mdab}}
    \boldsymbol{t}_b
    \cdot
    \hat{a}_{m+d_{ab},b}
  \Bigr]
