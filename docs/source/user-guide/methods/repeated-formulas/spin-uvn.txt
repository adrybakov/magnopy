.. math::
  \braket{\,u\,v\,n\,| \, S_{mi}\,}
    =\begin{pmatrix} S_{mi}^u \\ S_{mi}^v \\ S_{mi}^n \\\end{pmatrix}
    =S_i\,
     \begin{pmatrix}
            \sin\theta_i\cos(\phi_{mi}(q)) \\
            \sin\theta_i\sin(\phi_{mi}(q)) \\
            \cos\theta_i
     \end{pmatrix}

where

.. math::
  \phi_{mi}(q) = \boldsymbol{q}\cdot\boldsymbol{r_m} + \phi_i
               = \boldsymbol{q}\cdot\boldsymbol{r_{mi}} + \Delta\phi_i
